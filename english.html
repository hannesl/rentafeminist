Welcome to rent a feminist!

Background
Founded in 2006 with the purpose of developing and providing tools for increased agency, equality, participation and health of individuals and groups, rent a feminist offers a variety of interactive workshops.

The vision being to encourage the process of change and development within groups, individuals and - through this - society, the use of participatory methods is as fundamental to the workshops as are the themes.

 

Services
The following are some of the workshops available:

  • Gender and gender equality

  • Gender equality at the workplace

  • Empowerment, power structures and agency

  • Sisterhood

  • Methods for working with gender issues

The workshops are all developed for a group of 8 to 20 people, for a minimum of 1.5 hours. Previous participants include NGOs, youth organisations, teachers, etc.

 

About Jenny Wikman
The founder of rent a feminist, Jenny Wikman, is a devoted and much appreciated facilitator/trainer. She has a degree in gender studies, human rights and developmental studies, and practical experience of working within these fields as well as with participatory methods and empowerment.
